About Me

For me, my work is an opportunity to express what is important and meaningful. I am deeply in touch with my ideas and emotions and want a career that allows me to connect with a larger purpose. I am a naturally empathic and passionate individual.
"Why do you want to change career paths?"
My response, why not?
We are a constant evolving society, I am a person of growth and passion. The current career field that I am in is not fulfilling my passion or destiny in life. I started in healthcare in 2010 as a Pharmacy Technician at a retail pharmacy, it was my first job. I was able to become a State Certified Pharmacy Technician through on the job training and self-disciplined studying. I am currently still in pharmacy, but in a hospital setting now. I have been able to work in a few different environments, each uniquely their own.
After doing some critical thinking and processing what I wanted to do with the rest of my life, I knew I wanted to be involved in technology. That is when I decided I wanted to enter the field of Software Development. I want to be involved in the ever changing and evolving world of tech. It is such a challenging yet rewarding field. I love the challenging aspect of web development.
Both life and coding can be challenging, and challenge is an area I thrive in.
